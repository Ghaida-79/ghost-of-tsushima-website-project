Character

Jin Sakai

The last surviving samurai of Clan Sakai. As Tsushima falls to the Mongols, Jin adopts unconventional tactics and becomes known as the Ghost.

Lord Shimura

Jin Sakai's uncle and the Jito of Tsushima. He embodies traditional samurai honor and struggles with Jin's transformation.

Khotun Khan

The ruthless Mongol general leading the invasion of Tsushima. Intelligent and manipulative, he seeks to break the samurai's code.

Yuna

A skilled thief and survivor who helps Jin escape captivity. She encourages him to abandon tradition and do whatever it takes to save Tsushima.

Ryuzo

Leader of the Straw Hat Ronin and Jin's former friend. Torn between survival and honor, his choices lead him down a tragic path.

Lady Masako Adachi

A fierce samurai widow driven by revenge. Her journey explores grief, loyalty, and the cost of endless hatred.

Sensei Ishikawa

A legendary archer and former samurai mentor. He is strict, disciplined, and haunted by the mistakes of his past.

Norio

A warrior monk of Cedar Temple. He fights to protect the innocent while battling rage and despair within himself.

Kenji
the Merchant

A clever but unreliable sake trader who survives through lies and luck. Despite his cowardly nature, he occasionally helps Jin.

Gyozen
the Storyteller

A mysterious monk who narrates legendary tales of Tsushima. He guides warriors through mythic stories and the Legends mode.

Samurai Guardian

A devoted samurai tasked with protecting sacred lands and villages. He represents honor, duty, and the traditional code of the samurai.

Mongol Raider

A brutal invader who terrorizes villages across Tsushima. Raiders represent the chaos and cruelty of the Mongol invasion.
